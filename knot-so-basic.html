Our Story

More than yarn & a hook — it's handmade heart.

Knot So Basic was born from a love of colour, texture, and the slow, satisfying rhythm of crochet. Every piece we make is crafted by hand, stitch by stitch, with genuine care poured into each one.

We believe handmade goods carry something you just can't factory-produce — a warmth, a quirk, a little piece of the maker. Our style blends the cosy charm of cottagecore with playful bursts of colour, because why choose between pretty and fun?

Whether you're treating yourself or gifting someone special, everything from Knot So Basic is made to be loved and used — not just looked at.
